# Empathy Map: See & Say (Inbound Customers)

# What do they See?
 - Customers may find that competitors offer a better customer service experience
 - Customers prefer speaking with a person
 - Customers prefer having their issue, or query resolved quickly

# What do they Say?
- Customers may choose to take their business to a competitor that offers a more personalised call centre experience
- Customers will express their complaints and concerns about the current system in their reviews
- Customers feel that the current process is inefficient or ineffective
- Customers may not continue with the consultation if they feel their interaction has been negative
